About Crop Recommendation System 🌾

This project uses AI to recommend the best crop based on soil nutrients (Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium), weather conditions (temperature, humidity, rainfall), and pH value.

💡 Features:
  • Crop prediction using ML model
  • Weather-based recommendations
  • User-friendly interface
🚀 Goal:

Help farmers and users choose the right crop to increase productivity and reduce losses.
